About PsychSafe

PsychSafe is dedicated to providing accurate, science-based information about psychedelics with a focus on harm reduction and safety. Our goal is to help individuals make informed decisions and minimize risks.

While we recognize the potential therapeutic benefits being studied in clinical settings, we emphasize that recreational use carries risks that should be understood and respected.
